List:Buildings

Edit source
  • History
  • Talk (0)

Buildings need Energy to allow the clones to perform their work. The duration between recharges varies between buildings and can be seen on the "More" page of the info card when you click on a building. Energy is replenished by tapping on a building with the blue/lightning energy icon over it. During the working period the building will produce coins. Some buildings produce more coins than other buildings do. The Arrival Center however, does not produce any coins.

New buildings can be discovered by discovering new clones, you will need Land however.
